# Influencer Database

A watchlist of influencers/experts whose names carry search demand in this brand's niche. **Only used for influencer-style posts** (style 3 in `blog-styles.md`). The other six styles ignore this file. Fork-friendly: fill it with names relevant to the brand.

## How to use it
1. When the chosen post is influencer-style, pick one name from the table below.
2. Validate the name's search volume in Keyword Planner (name, "[name] products", "[name] tips") against the threshold in `config.md`.
3. Mine 2 to 3 of their top videos / a transcript (use the `youtube-transcript` skill) for what they recommend.
4. Map 3 to 5 of our products to their topics, then write to the influencer-style structure.

## Guardrails (non-negotiable)
- Reference only **real, public** figures and only their **publicly-known** methods/philosophy.
- **Never** fabricate quotes, endorsements, or partnerships, and never imply the figure endorses the brand or product. Frame as "applying [Name]'s widely-taught method", not "[Name] recommends [Product]".
- No defamatory or unverifiable claims.
