# 3 Reasons Why
**Status: LOCKED (2026-06-19).** A fast, punchy video (**up to 30s**) that makes the value obvious by stacking **exactly three** sharp, specific reasons. Great for warmer audiences weighing it up, and for paid ads that need to land quickly. Read `00-engine.md` first.

## Why it works
It's a clean, satisfying structure the viewer can feel the shape of — a promise, then three quick pay-offs, then a nudge. Each reason re-hooks attention, so retention stays high. The trick: reasons must be **specific and benefit-led**, not feature bullets.

## Hook logic (0–3s)
Lead with the number + a bold/contrarian claim or the exact person. Examples of the shape: "Three reasons I'll never go back to [old way]." / "If you [situation], here are three reasons [product] is about to fix your week." No "Have you ever." ## Beats
| Beat | ~Time | What to do |
|---|---|---|
| 1 — Hook | 3s | The number + the promise/claim. Say who it's for. |
| 2 — Reason 1 (strongest) | 5s | Your single biggest benefit, stated as an outcome + one proof/specific. Lead with your best — don't save it. |
| 3 — Reason 2 | 5s | A different *axis* of value (e.g. if R1 = taste, R2 = convenience). Keep one concrete detail. |
| 4 — Reason 3 (the clincher) | 5s | The objection-killer or the surprising one (price-per-use, sustainability, "and it actually…"). |
| 5 — The ask | 4s | One line: who it's for + the single action. |

**Always pull the three reasons from the brand's ranked messaging pillars (brand-data.md §5)** — take the three *strongest, on-strategy* ones (fall back to `config.md` if there's no profile). **Hard three — never two, never four.** If two pillars cover the same angle, pick the stronger and move down the ranking so the three feel distinct. Put each reason as **[on-screen text]** too — this format reads well muted.

## Worked example sketch (generic — replace)
Hook: "Three reasons I stopped buying [category] at the shop." → R1: "It's [outcome] — [proof]." → R2: "Takes [time], no [hassle]." → R3: "And it's [clincher / objection flip]." → Ask: "If you [situation], give it a go — link's right there."

## Length
One script, **capped at 30 seconds**. Beats below land ~22–28s at normal pace — that's the target. For a paid placement you can tighten each reason to hit ~15s, but the default deliverable is a single version up to 30s (don't auto-produce two cuts).

## Shoot notes
Punchy energy, quicker cuts than the Founder Ad. Count on fingers / hold the product for each reason. On-screen text per reason. Up to 30s total.
