# Paid Ads Plan

Ads run the same offer as the inbox and the website, phased across the funnel. The job is to capture a warm list before the sale, then convert at launch and through the window. Ad accounts and any connector details live in `campaign-config.md`.

Funnel shorthand: **TOF** = top of funnel (cold/prospecting), **MOF** = middle (warm — engagers, site visitors, video viewers), **BOF** = bottom (hot — add-to-cart, past purchasers, list members).

## Meta + TikTok

Three ad beats, mapped to the timeline phases.

### Beat 1 — Early Bird Sign-Up (TOF) — capture phase
- **When:** the 1–2 weeks before public launch.
- **Goal:** drive cold traffic to the early-access sign-up page/popup; build the Early-Access list.
- **Audience:** cold prospecting (interest/lookalike/broad per the account).
- **Angle:** "Something's coming. Get first dibs on [event] — sign up for early access." Tease the offer without fully revealing it, or reveal it if the strategy is openness.
- **Creative:** anticipation-led — product hero + "early access" framing. Short hook in the first frame for TikTok.
- **CTA:** Sign Up / Learn More → capture page.

### Beat 2 — Launch Offer (TOF + MOF) — go-live
- **When:** public launch day, running through the sale.
- **Goal:** announce the live sale to cold (TOF) and warm (MOF) audiences.
- **Audience:** TOF prospecting + MOF (site visitors, engagers, video viewers, early-access signups who didn't buy).
- **Angle:** the offer, stated plainly — "[Headline offer] is on. [reason]. Ends [date]."
- **Creative:** the offer visible in the first frame / on the image; price or mechanic clear; brand-true (not clearance-bin).
- **CTA:** Shop Now → sale collection.

### Beat 3 — Sale + Catalog (MOF + BOF) — through the sale
- **When:** from launch to close, intensifying toward the end.
- **Goal:** convert warm/hot audiences; retarget browsers and cart-abandoners with the actual products.
- **Audience:** MOF (engagers, visitors) + BOF (add-to-cart, initiate-checkout, past purchasers, list members).
- **Creative:** dynamic product/catalog ads (DPA) showing the discounted SKUs with sale pricing; plus an "ends soon" urgency creative for the final 48h.
- **CTA:** Shop Now / Shop the Sale → product or collection.

## Google

Two updates — quick to do, high leverage during a sale.

### Update headlines & copy
- Refresh Search ad headlines and descriptions to lead with the sale ("[X]% off — ends [date]", the code if used).
- Update any responsive search assets so the offer shows in rotation.
- Revert to evergreen copy at close.

### Add sitelinks & promotional extensions
- Add **promotion extensions** (Google's native sale/discount callout) with the offer + dates.
- Add/refresh **sitelinks** pointing at the sale collection and top categories.
- Add **callout extensions** if useful ("Ends [date]", "Free shipping", etc.).

## For each ad, the plan should give

- **Beat / funnel stage** (e.g. "Launch Offer — TOF+MOF")
- **Audience**
- **Primary text options** (2–3 for Meta/TikTok)
- **Headline options**
- **Creative direction** (what's in the visual, the offer treatment, the hook for video)
- **CTA + destination**

## Rules
- **Ads must match the inbox and site** — same offer, same code, same dates.
- **Confirm ads are live at launch** (it's an explicit timeline step) — a launch email with no supporting ads leaves money on the table.
- **Watch frequency / ROAS** mid-sale; kill underperformers, scale winners (timeline step "Check ads & optimise").
- **Turn capture ads off** once the public sale launches; turn all sale ads off at close.
